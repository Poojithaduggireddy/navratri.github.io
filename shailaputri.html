Shailaputri Devi

The avatar of Shailaputri Maa during Navaratri symbolizes women's empowerment in several ways. Shailaputri, the daughter of the Himalayas, represents strength, resilience, and determination. Her journey to self-discovery and self-realization signifies the inner power that women possess. By embarking on a quest and choosing her own path, Shailaputri exemplifies the importance of women's independence and the pursuit of their goals. Her story serves as an inspirational reminder that women have the ability to overcome obstacles and achieve greatness, reinforcing the message of empowerment during Navaratri.

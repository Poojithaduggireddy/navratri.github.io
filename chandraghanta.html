Chandraghanta Devi

The Chandraghanta avatar of Navaratri has profound implications for women's empowerment. She is depicted with a third eye, symbolizing intuitive insight and foresight. This empowers women to trust their instincts and assert their views confidently. Her name, "Chandraghanta," signifies a half-moon shaped like a bell, representing the elimination of negative forces and the importance of inner strength. This avatar inspires women to be fearless and resolute in the face of adversity, fostering a sense of self-assuredness and empowerment. Chandraghanta's image serves as a reminder of the inner warrior within women, instilling courage and self-belief on their path to empowerment.
